About
RECWALL is the project of singer-guitarist Robert Emmet Christopher Wall — guitar-driven melodic hard rock with deep roots in the 90s hard rock era. Wall's musical history runs from the NYC club scene through major label showcases with Zen Bender in the early 90s, through the hard rock outfit As It Iz, and into a prolific solo run that has produced nine albums since 2017. The most recent, Singularity (2025), is his most focused and deliberate work to date. Dave Bania on bass and Bob Lewis on drums round out the live unit, with guitarist and producer Trace Davis contributing to select recordings along the way.
